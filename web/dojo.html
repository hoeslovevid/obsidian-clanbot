Clan HQ

Dojo operations

Keep the in-game dojo and your Discord aligned — research, roles, and squad coordination.

Research & labs

  • Prioritize key research (forma, weapons, arcanes) before cosmetic rooms.
  • Track progress with /dojo_research in Discord — add items and update contributions.
  • Post weekly “donate” reminders in your resources channel; link required mats from Acquire.

Decorations & layout

  • Keep a clear path from spawn to trading post and labs.
  • Use /dojo_decoration to log who placed what — helps when rotating decorators.
  • Screenshot your layout for new members; pin in Discord.

Roles & ranks

  • Architect — dojo build rights; limit to trusted officers.
  • Knight / Sage — research and treasury access as your clan prefers.
  • Mirror important ranks in Discord so mods can ping the right group.

Discord tools

  • /lfg — squad up for sorties, archon hunts, and key runs. See LFG guide.
  • /events — schedule clan nights and dojo decorating sessions. See Events guide.
  • /ticket — disputes, account issues, or officer requests without public drama.

Officer checklist

  1. Audit inactive members before rank decay hits.
  2. Confirm alert channels still work — run /baro or check world state.
  3. Open the dashboard for inbox and setup health.
