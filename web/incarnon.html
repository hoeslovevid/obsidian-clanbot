Popular Incarnons

Build direction at a glance. Evolution choices depend on your mods, Riven, frame buffs, and mission; treat these as starting points.

Genesis primaries

  • Boar — lean into multishot/status and its chaining form. Farm · Market
  • Braton — favor base damage and critical consistency. Farm · Market
  • Paris — projectile and critical choices reward aimed headshots. Farm · Market
  • Soma — sustain critical scaling and magazine uptime. Farm · Market
  • Torid — status/critical hybrid; prioritize comfortable beam uptime. Farm · Market

Genesis secondaries

  • Lato — critical and ricochet-focused choices scale well. Farm · Market
  • Lex — emphasize critical damage and punch-through waves. Farm · Market
  • Dual Toxocyst — headshot activation, fire rate, and status hybrid. Farm · Market

Zariman weapons

  • Laetum — choose non-critical attrition or critical scaling; do not mix blindly. Farm · Market
  • Phenmor — non-critical attrition is the common general-purpose route. Farm · Market
  • Felarx — attrition builds favor low critical chance and raw multishot. Farm · Market
  • Innodem — select mobility or finisher perks to fit your melee loop. Farm · Market
