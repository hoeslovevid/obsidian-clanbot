First week as Tenno

A lean checklist for new players in your clan — star chart progress, resources, and Discord habits.

Star chart path

  1. Earth — learn movement, melee, and modding. Clear Vor’s prize.
  2. Venus — introduce ranged play and resource diversity.
  3. Junctions — each planet boss unlocks the next relay. Bring the required MR gear; ask clan for taxi if stuck.

Pin world state for fissures and alerts once you reach Mars and beyond.

Early resources

  • Neurodes — many early blueprints; Earth and Lua are common farms.
  • Morphics — Mercury and Mars drop tables; check Acquire for live nodes.
  • Always pick up Forma alerts when they appear — you will need them.

Discord habits

  • Run /daily in your clan server for coins and streak rewards.
  • Use /lfg when you need a squad — faster than pinging general.
  • Read pinned setup posts so you know which channels are for alerts vs chat.

Join a clan

Clans unlock research, trading tax discounts, and dojo keys. Look for an active dojo with low inactivity penalty. Introduce yourself, ask for an invite to voice during an event, and link your Warframe account if the server supports it.
