How grading works

Each Riven stat is base × disposition (ω) × config weight × roll (90–110%). We back out the roll factor from your entered value and map it to a letter grade. Overall grade averages the positives.

Weekly plat averages come from Digital Extremes’ public trade snapshot (unrolled vs rerolled). Your exact roll will differ.
