Trading guide

Warframe Market etiquette, price checks, and how our site fits in — read-only lookup, trade in game.

Warframe Market etiquette

  • Whisper first — confirm item, rank, and price before inviting to dojo.
  • “Invite” means ready — be in dojo or relay when you say you are.
  • No ghosting — if you cannot trade, say so; cancel the listing if needed.
  • MR 2+ required to trade; clan tax applies in dojo — factor that into prices.

Use our Market page

Market is a read-only lookup — search an item, compare buy/sell orders, then open warframe.market to place trades. Great for quick price checks in Discord without leaving the browser.

In Discord, /trading commands can search listings and configure your clan’s trading channel.

Platinum tips

  • Farm tradeable parts with Acquire before buying — primes and vaulted mods add up.
  • Check historical lows on WFM; undercutting by 1p is normal, dumping hurts everyone.
  • Never buy plat from third-party sellers — account risk and ToS violation.
  • Stock up on Forma and catalysts when you have spare plat during sales.

Before you list

  1. Search the item on Market.
  2. Confirm you have the correct rank (mods, arcanes, rivens).
  3. Set a competitive price; refresh if it sits for days.
