Enemy intel

Faction cheat sheet

What to bring against each faction. Community-standard tips — not gospel for every mission type.

Grineer

Heavy armor · weak to Corrosive and Radiation

Ferrite/ alloy armor scales with level. Strip armor first, then slash or heat.

  • Status: Corrosive, Viral (after armor strip), Heat
  • Mods: Corrosive Projection, Shield of Shadows less relevant
  • Watch: rollers, bombards, napalms at higher tiers

Salvage · Alloy Plate · Detonite Injector

Corpus

Shields first · weak to Magnetic and Toxin

Shield-heavy. Magnetic procs help; toxin bypasses shields on health.

  • Status: Magnetic, Toxin, Viral on flesh
  • Nullifiers and combas strip abilities — bring amp or brute force
  • Robotics: Radiation for confusion

Polymer Bundle · Control Module · Oxium

Infested

Flesh · weak to Heat and Gas

Swarms with toxic auras. Range and AoE shine; ancients can disrupt.

  • Status: Heat, Gas, Corrosive on ancients
  • Healing: bring frames that resist or cleanse toxin

Nano Spores · Mutagen Mass · Forma (Hieracon)

Corrupted

Mixed armor + shields · bring both counters

Void towers and fissures. Expect shielded heavies and armored units together.

  • Status: Corrosive + Magnetic or Viral combo
  • Relic rewards — check world state for fissures

Void Traces · prime parts via fissures

Sentient

Adaptive resistances · rotate elements

Eidolons and railjack sentients adapt to repeated damage types. Cycle weapons and elements.

  • Operator amp for shields, then match element to weak point
  • Void damage resets adaptations — Operator / Paracesis

Eidolon Shard · Breath of the Eidolon

Murmur / Man in the Wall

Zariman · void anomalies

Angels and holdfast missions. Void and slash builds common; check current community guides for arcane swaps.

  • Bring reliable survivability — burst damage windows matter
  • Archon shards and incarnon weapons are popular here

Narmer

Amalgam + corrupted mix · veilbreaker

Narmer missions blend factions. Armor strip and shield break both help.

  • Status: flexible — Corrosive, Magnetic, Heat
  • Archon mods drop from weekly hunts

Archon Shard
