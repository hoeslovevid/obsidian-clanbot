There is no official Warframe build API. Community sites host loadouts — we link out and help you find parts.

Where to search

Overframe is the go-to for published builds. Open the site and use its search bar for warframes, weapons, and arcanes. Warframe Builder (warframe-builder.com) is another solid planner if you prefer step-by-step mod fitting.

Starter ideas — early game

Rhino Tank aura, Iron Skin — farm blueprint and neurodes. Mag Magnetic pull — good for grouped enemies and index prep. Volt Speed and shields — fast capture and low MR missions.

Steel Path starters

Once you unlock Steel Path, these frames/weapons are forgiving. Check Archon shards and Honoria rewards next.

Revenant Mesmer Shield — very tanky for SP content. Wisp Haste mote + health — great for squads and survival. Kuva Bramma AoE clear — farm Kuva Liches; watch self-damage.
